    Welcome to Arena Fight

    Arena Fight is a Battle-Arena Fighting Game in the vein of Super Smash Bros. The project came about while playing the Super Smash Bros 4 demo. I was curious as to how other game universes would play in a smash bros style.

    I also wanted to make this as the smash bros style gameplay is my favorite style of gameplay.

    Development recently began and is not yet in a playable state.
